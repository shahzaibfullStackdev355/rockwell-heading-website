Our Story

Wade and Vangel Roberts launched Wade Roberts Plumbing in 2004 with a vision to create a plumbing company that did more than just fix pipes. They set out to build strong connections in their community with others who shared their belief in a world where everyone has access to safe drinking water.

Once a philosopher and soul-searching surfer, Wade travelled extensively in Asia and India and was impacted by the number of people he saw who lacked this basic access to something we all take for granted.

One filter or one well may feel like a drop in a bucket, but Wade and Vangel determined to be a part of the solution, and set out to build a team who would do just that. It has been a long and bumpy road, with many setbacks along the way, but their passion continues to grow and they are excited for what is ahead.

Our Philosophy

Wade Roberts Plumbing strives to build their business one client at a time by providing exceptional service. They believe that a great business is built on the loyalty of its clients and referrals, and they are committed to excelling as a service company for your plumbing, gas-fitting and backflow prevention needs. Wade Roberts Plumbing is fully licensed, bonded and insured, making them the perfect choice for quality service and products. They provide honest, personalized service that ensures you years of comfort knowing that the job was done right.

Our Vision

Building Strong Connections in our Community and Around the World

At Wade Roberts Plumbing, we believe plumbing is a powerful tool to bring about good in the world and we plan to do just that.


We care deeply for the wellbeing of our community, and we believe in giving back to the city that supports us. We are dedicated to a sustainable future for our city, and our entire team donates their time, talent and resources to a variety of charities who are committed to making Victoria healthier for those in need.


The water crisis in our world effects 785 million people, including our own Indigenous communities right here in Canada. We're on a mission to change that.


Access to clean water and basic sanitation can save lives, and simple solutions like water filtration systems and community wells give communities more time to grow food, earn an income and go to school - all of which fight poverty.


Our Team believes in a world where everyone has access to safe drinking water, and we are dedicated to donating 10% of our profits to water projects that will make a difference.
